WELCOME TO SPURTHI
RURAL DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY

SPURTHI RURAL DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY is a voluntary organization registered under societies Registration Act I of 1350 F vide no 91/1997 dated 08.01.1997. And 12A, & 80G Income Tax exemptions, Since its inception rendering services for the benefit of below poverty line providing upgrade skill development, women Entrepreneurship, Health, Education, Rural Development and human, consumer rights cultural awareness activities, Road Safety and Environment.

Vision: SRDS initiate development interventions for the up liftment of poor and marginal communities, socially, economically self reliant and conscious about basic rights and human dignity.

Mission: SRDS is working and assist the needy neglected people at gross root level and find access to sustain in the society with due ginity. Therefore SRDS will takeall necessary initiatives/ measurer towords empoerment and skill development self-determinations of community people enabling them to slove their own socio-economic problems by themselves towards poverty elevation.

Goal: SRDS main Goal rural development, economic, empowerment, poor and marginal communities.to create across to oppurtunities and benefits for their will being conduct of training programmes for women and youth tocreate awareness promote and provide education livelihood and capacity builiding skills towords social, economical and cultural empower of the people.
